Cross Stitch Pattern Maker

For Grandma

Instructions

1. Upload an image by clicking on the browse button below and selecting an image.

2. You can slide the sliders below that to adjust the amount of stitches per inch and the amount of different coloured threads you'll need.

3. You can press the toggle grid button to disable or enable the overlaying grid.

4. Once you're happy with the image, you can download it by clicking the download button to either print it out or come back to it another day.

Notes: It tends to work better on a laptop, if you want to more accurately slide the sliders you can click it once and then use the arrow keys.
